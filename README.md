## Current Projects

### [MEGATEK.AI](https://megatek.ai) — World AI Directory
The world's most multilingual AI directory. Lists software, websites, apps, YouTube channels, Discord servers and browser extensions. Articles written by human editors and AI, automatically translated and published in **100 languages**.

### [MindChat World](https://mindchat.world) — AI Conversational Library
Chat with **150+ historical minds** from literature, philosophy and science. Covers 25 centuries of human thought across all continents, in **16 languages**. Freemium SaaS with embeddable widgets.

### [Aria Coach](https://aria-coach.chat) — AI Anti-Smoking Coach
A motivational AI coach supporting people who want to quit smoking. Available **24/7 in 6 languages**, with a structured 8-module program, persistent memory and statistical tracking. Scientific documentation reviewed by **Pr Bertrand Dautzenberg** (Sorbonne University).
